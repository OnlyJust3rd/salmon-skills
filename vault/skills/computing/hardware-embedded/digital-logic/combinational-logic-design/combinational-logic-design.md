# Combinational Logic Design

Utilize Karnaugh Maps (K-maps) to optimize and minimize multi-level combinational logic circuits. Analyze and design modular combinational logic circuits (including adders, subtractors, multiplexers, and decoders), and verify their logical behavior and correctness using Proteus 8. Translate real-life problems into truth functions and conceptualize digital circuit models to address those specific problems.
