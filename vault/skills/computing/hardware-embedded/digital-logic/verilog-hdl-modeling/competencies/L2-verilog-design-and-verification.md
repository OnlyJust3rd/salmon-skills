# L3- Verilog Design and Verification

Parent macro: [[skills/computing/hardware-embedded/digital-logic/verilog-hdl-modeling/verilog-hdl-modeling|Verilog HDL Modeling]]

## Competency Statement

Model, implement, and verify combinational and sequential digital circuits using Verilog HDL at different levels of abstraction, including behavioral modeling, and develop testbenches.
