Parent macro: [[internet-of-things|Internet of Things]]

## Competency Statement

Describe and analyze the current state of research, emerging trends, and key characteristics of emerging IoT technologies such as Digital Twins, 5G, Edge AI/TinyML, and Industry 4.0, and interpret their impact, including sustainability concerns.
