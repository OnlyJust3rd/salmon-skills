# L1- Define and Identify Text Representations

Parent macro: [[skills/computing/data-ai/data-science/text-feature-engineering/text-feature-engineering|Text Feature Engineering]]

## Competency Statement

Define and identify discrete and distributed text representation methods, including One-Hot encoding, Bag-of-Words (BoW), TF-IDF, Co-Occurrence matrices, Word2Vec (CBOW and Skip-Gram), and GloVe.
