# Cryptographic Fundamentals

Understanding and articulating the fundamental theoretical underpinnings and goals of cryptographic systems, including secrecy, authentication, and integrity. This includes defining the goals of selective encryption for multimedia communications and describing the properties of cryptographic one-way hash functions, explaining how they map variable-length data to fixed-length digests.
