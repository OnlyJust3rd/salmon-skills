# Symmetric Cipher Operations

Analyzing the step-by-step operations of modern block ciphers like DES, IDEA, and AES, including their internal processes like SubBytes, ShiftRows, MixColumns, and AddRoundKey. This also includes describing the mechanics of stream ciphers, specifically Linear Feedback Shift Registers (LFSR), combining bit-stream generators (like Geffe), and the unbreakable One-time Pad.
