Parent macro: [[skills/professional-practice/human-centered-design/human-cognitive-and-perception/human-cognitive-and-perception|Human Cognitive and Perception]]

## Competency Statement

Explain how human cognitive processes, limitations, perceptual capabilities, and principles of visual hierarchy affect user perception, attention, and interaction with interactive systems and influence experimental design.
