# L2- Geometric Interpretation of Line and Plane Parameters

Parent macro: [[geometric-formulation|Geometric Formulation]]

## Competency Statement

Explain the geometric interpretations of parameters within the equations of lines and planes in 3D space, including the role of direction vectors for lines and normal vectors for planes.
