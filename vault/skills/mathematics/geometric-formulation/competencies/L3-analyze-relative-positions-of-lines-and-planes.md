# L4- Analyze Relative Positions of Lines and Planes

Parent macro: [[geometric-formulation|Geometric Formulation]]

## Competency Statement

Analyze the relative positions of lines and planes in 3D space by examining their direction vectors and normal vectors to identify parallelism, orthogonality, and conditions for intersection.
