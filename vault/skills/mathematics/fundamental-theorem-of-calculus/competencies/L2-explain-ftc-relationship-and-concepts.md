# L2- Explain FTC Relationship and Concepts

Parent macro: [[fundamental-theorem-of-calculus|Fundamental Theorem of Calculus]]

## Competency Statement

Explain the relationship between differentiation and integration as described by the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, including its role in defining new functions and the basic concepts of integrals.
