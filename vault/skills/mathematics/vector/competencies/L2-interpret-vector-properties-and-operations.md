# L2- Interpret Vector Properties and Operations

Parent macro: [[vector|Vector]]

## Competency Statement

Explain the geometric and algebraic interpretations of vector products, and the geometric meaning of vector magnitude, unit vectors, angles between vectors, and orthogonal projections, as well as how direction vectors define line orientation.
