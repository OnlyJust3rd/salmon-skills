# Multivariable Calculus Analysis

Analyze functions of several variables, including sketching graphs of equations, evaluating limits and continuity, computing first and second-order partial derivatives and differentials, applying the chain rule, and identifying critical points and relative extrema (maxima, minima, saddle points).
