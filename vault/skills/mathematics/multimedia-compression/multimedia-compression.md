# Multimedia Compression

Describing the major steps of the JPEG image compression standard (DCT, Quantization, Zig-zag scan, Entropy coding) and comparing its operation modes (Sequential, Lossless, Progressive, Hierarchical). This also includes explaining video compression concepts, focusing on spatial and temporal redundancy removal via Intra-frames (I) and Inter-frames (P, B) using motion vectors in the MPEG and H.261/H.263 standards, and summarizing audio compression techniques, including psychoacoustics (frequency and temporal masking) and the subband filtering process used in MPEG Audio.
