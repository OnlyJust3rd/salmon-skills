# L3- Apply Transform Calculations

Parent macro: [[complex-frequency-domain-analysis|Complex Frequency Domain Analysis]]

## Competency Statement

Calculate and determine the poles, zeros, and Region of Convergence (ROC) for given Laplace and Z-transform functions and transfer functions, and compute inverse Z-transforms using long division.
