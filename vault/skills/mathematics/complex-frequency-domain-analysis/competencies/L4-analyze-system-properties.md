# L4- Analyze System Properties

Parent macro: [[complex-frequency-domain-analysis|Complex Frequency Domain Analysis]]

## Competency Statement

Analyze the causality and stability of continuous-time and discrete-time systems by examining the locations of poles and zeros and their associated ROCs on pole-zero plots and transfer functions.
