# L3- Compute Fundamental Subspaces and Dimensions

Parent macro: [[subspaces-and-orthogonality|Subspaces and Orthogonality]]

## Competency Statement

Compute the row space, column space, null space, kernel, and range of a given matrix or linear transformation, and determine their dimensions (rank and nullity).
