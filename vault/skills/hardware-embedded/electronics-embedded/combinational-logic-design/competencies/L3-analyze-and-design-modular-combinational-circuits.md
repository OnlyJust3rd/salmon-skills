# L4- Analyze and Design Modular Combinational Circuits

Parent macro: [[skills/hardware-embedded/electronics-embedded/combinational-logic-design/combinational-logic-design|Combinational Logic Design]]

## Competency Statement

Analyze and design modular combinational logic circuits (including adders, subtractors, multiplexers, and decoders), and verify their logical behavior and correctness using Proteus 8.
