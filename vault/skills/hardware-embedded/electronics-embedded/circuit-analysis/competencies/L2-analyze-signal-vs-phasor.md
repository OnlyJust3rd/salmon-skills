Parent macro: [[skills/hardware-embedded/electronics-embedded/circuit-analysis/circuit-analysis|Circuit Analysis]]

## Competency Statement

Apply the conversion formula to transform given sinusoidal voltages and currents into their phasor equivalents and analyze the impact of changes in amplitude and phase on the phasor representation.
