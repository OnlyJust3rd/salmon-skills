# L3- Analyze Step Responses

Parent macro: [[skills/hardware-embedded/electronics-embedded/circuit-analysis/circuit-analysis|Circuit Analysis]]

## Competency Statement

Compute the time constant and analyze the step response of RC and RL circuits based on their component values and the relationship between circuit components, time constants, and the resulting voltage or current waveforms over time.
