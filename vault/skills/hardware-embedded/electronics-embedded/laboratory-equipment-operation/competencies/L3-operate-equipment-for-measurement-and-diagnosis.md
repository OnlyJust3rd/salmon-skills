# L3- Operate Equipment for Measurement and Diagnosis

Parent macro: [[skills/hardware-embedded/electronics-embedded/laboratory-equipment-operation/laboratory-equipment-operation|Laboratory Equipment Operation]]

## Competency Statement

Safely operate standard laboratory equipment to measure specific circuit parameters (e.g., voltage, current, signal integrity) and test functionality to verify power supply, ensure continuity, and diagnose physical hardware challenges.
