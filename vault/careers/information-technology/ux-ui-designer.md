# UX/UI Designer

## Description

An UX/UI Designer is an entry-level information technology role focused on applying the listed competencies in practical product, platform, or operational contexts.

- Apply [[skills/professional-practice/human-centered-design/user-centered-design/competencies/L1-describe-ucd-process-and-user-needs|L2- Describe UCD Process and User Needs]] in day-to-day role work.

- Apply [[skills/professional-practice/human-centered-design/user-experience/competencies/L1-ux-workflow-elements|L1- UX Workflow Elements]] in day-to-day role work.
- Apply [[skills/professional-practice/human-centered-design/user-experience/competencies/L2-design-ux-flows|L3- Design UX Flows]] in day-to-day role work.
- Apply [[skills/professional-practice/human-centered-design/user-experience/competencies/L2-conduct-solution-interviews-and-usability-testing|L3- Conduct Solution Interviews and Usability Testing]] in day-to-day role work.
